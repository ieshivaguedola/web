Mission and Values

Our goal is to form:

  • Jews with a firm life commitment to the eternal legacy, tradition, and the mission of the Jewish people
  • Bachurim academically prepared with the capacity to successfully function in the modern world materially and spiritually
  • Tmimim who will continuously contribute to the well-being of the community
  • Chasidim who selflessly assume leadership with vision and conviction

We are achieving these goals by:

  • Choosing highly qualified academics and experienced teachers in Torah
  • Sustaining an amazing infrastructure
  • Granting scholarships to promising future leaders
